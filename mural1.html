Vera Bugatti (Italy) and Carlos Alberto Hernandez (Mexico)

Alice in Wonderland, 2016

The most surprising elements in the work of art are
the left foot of Alice, the claws of the rabbit, Alice's
eyes and the cake in her hand, which can be
reached regardless of your position to the wall. "Eat
me" is an essential part of the fairy tale, since Alice
enlarged or reduces her size when eating the cake.
Think about it - Alice is a symbol of the ups
and downs in life.
